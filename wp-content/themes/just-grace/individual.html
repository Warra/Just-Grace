Individuals

Sponsor a learner

We have created a unique way for individuals to contribute to our education programme! You will be able to sponsor a learner for the Just Grace education programme, giving them the opportunity to receive additional tutoring, career guidance, mentorship, computer training and job readiness skills. You will be provided with an annual progress report on the learners, allowing you to follow their progress at school and in the programme. We have calculated the estimated cost per learner as: R800. This is the cost of the core education programme components divided by the number of annual learners.

Download a copy of our sponsor a leaner document, which includes a cost breakdown, a list of unsponsored learners, and our banking details.

Mercy Fund

Many of our learners come from impoverished homes and from time to time the need arises for external support to be given to a learner (the learner may have no parent or guardian with sufficient income to provide adequate food/shelter etc.) Please consider donating to our Mercy fund, or alternatively leave us with your contact details and we can inform you when an opportunity arises to provide mercy support.

Alternatively, make a general donation into our bank account and help Just Grace to continue to have an impact in the Langa community.
